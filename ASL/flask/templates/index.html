ASL Predictor

This application uses deep learning to recognize and predict American Sign Language (ASL) gestures from images, helping users communicate more effectively.

Our model can recognize all 26 letters of the alphabet in ASL, making it a valuable tool for learning and communication.

Upload an Image & Get Prediction
How Image Processing Works:

Image Upload

When you upload an ASL gesture image, our application securely stores it for processing.

Image Preprocessing

The image is resized to 64×64 pixels and converted to a format suitable for our neural network.

Deep Learning Analysis

Our VGG16-based CNN model analyzes the processed image, extracting features that identify the ASL gesture.

Prediction Generation

The model outputs probabilities for all 26 letters (A-Z) and selects the most likely letter as the prediction.

Result Display

The predicted letter is displayed on the screen, helping you interpret the ASL gesture.
